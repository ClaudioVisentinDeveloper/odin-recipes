Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a type of Italian dish made with a flat, round base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 500g flour
  • 300ml water
  • 10g salt
  • 7g yeast
  • 200g tomato sauce
  • 200g mozzarella cheese
  • 100g pepperoni
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of oregano

Steps

  1. Dissolve the yeast in the water.
  2. Add the flour and salt to the water and mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  4. Let the dough rise for 1 hour.
  5. Preheat the oven to 250Â°C.
  6. Roll out the dough into a circle.
  7. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough.
  8. Top with the mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, onion, and bell pepper.
  9. Sprinkle with sugar and oregano.
  10. Drizzle with olive oil.
  11. Bake in the oven for 15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
