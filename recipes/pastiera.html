Pastiera Recipe

Description

Pastiera is a type of Italian cake made with ricotta cheese and candied fruit. It is usually eaten at Easter.

Ingredients

  • 500g ricotta cheese
  • 200g sugar
  • 100g candied fruit
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 orange
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon of orange blossom water
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of milk
  • 1 packet of puff pastry

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Grease a cake tin with butter and dust with flour.
  3. Roll out the puff pastry and line the cake tin with it.
  4. Beat the eggs with the sugar until light and fluffy.
  5. Add the ricotta cheese, candied fruit, vanilla extract, orange blossom water, and the zest of the lemon and orange.
  6. Pour the mixture into the cake tin.
  7. Roll out the remaining puff pastry and cut into strips.
  8. Arrange the strips in a lattice pattern on top of the cake.
  9. Brush the top of the cake with milk.
  10. Bake in the oven for 45 minutes or until golden brown.
